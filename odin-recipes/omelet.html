Deena Omelet Special

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Pam

Steps

  1. Spray pan with Pam.
  2. Break 4 egg and place eggs whites in pan with 1 egg yoke.
  3. Salt and pepper teh eggs, add cheddar cheese.
  4. Cook on low heat, do not mix. Once it reached desired doneness serve and enjoy.
