Moochi Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Rice Flour
  • 1 (13.5-oz.) Coconut Milk
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar
  • 1 tsp. Cardamon
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • Coconut flakes
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • Btuter

Steps

  1. Mix together dry ingredients into mixing bowl. Rice flour, baking powder salt, sugar, and cardamon. TIP: Sift rice flour through wire mesh to avoid clumps.
  2. Preheat ovent to 350 Degrees farenhiet.
  3. Cover cooking pan with butter. Spread sugar overthe buttered pan.
  4. Mix together sugar and butter in another bowl. Once mixed, add vanillia extract and each egg one at a time.
  5. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients, mix until no clumps remain.
  6. Pour mixture into baking dish. Cover with conconut flakes.
  7. Bake for 45-55 minutes.
  8. Let cook and enjoy!
