Greek Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Mix together spices and cover chicken, ensure to get under chicken skin.
  2. Let Chicken Marinate for 30-40 mins.
  3. Bake chicken at 400 degrees for 30 mins and till internal temperature reaches 165 Degree F.
  4. Enjoy!
